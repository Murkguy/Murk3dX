# THE PANTHEON

The gods of Tellus are not myths. They answer. They judge. They withhold.

Eight Patrons are known. Each embodies a different answer to the same question: *what do you worship when the world ends?* Their names are spoken in the Murk, in the factories, in the ruins of the old temples. Whether they listen depends entirely on what you have done in their name.

---

* **[Makru (The Iron Judge)](MAKRU.md)** — God of walls, law, and stasis.
* **[The Penitent Choir (The Mourners)](THE_PENITENT_CHOIR.md)** — Collective spirits of the First Martyrs.
* **[The Nine Mothers (The Tursakkaanen)](THE_NINE_MOTHERS.md)** — Goddesses of fate, nature, and the cycle.
* **[The Charred One (The Mad God)](THE_CHARRED_ONE.md)** — Aspects: entropy, fire, nihilism.
* **[Xelas (The Younger Brother)](XELAS.md)** — Aspects: knowledge, creation, light.
* **[Larantas (The Great Tree)](LARANTAS.md)** — Aspects: the green, the rot, symbiosis.
* **[Apatur (The War God)](APATUR.md)** — Aspects: struggle, dominion, iron.
* **[The Midnight Lady (The Veiled Matron)](THE_MIDNIGHT_LADY.md)** — Aspects: secrets, shadows, the void.
