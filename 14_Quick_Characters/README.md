# CHARACTER CREATION QUICKSHEET

## 1. ASSIGN ATTRIBUTES
Distribute the following dice to **Body**, **Mind**, and **Spirit**:
* **d10** (Primary)
* **d8** (Secondary)
* **d6** (Weakness)

---

## 2. CHOOSE ARCHETYPE
*Gain Unique Attribute (d8), Resource, and 3 Intrinsic Perks. Also choose 1 Tier 1 Talent from your Archetype tree.*

### A. [THE SOLDIER](/02_Character_Creation/01_Archetypes/SOLDIER/) (Tactics d8)
* **Resource:** Command Points (CP) — Roll Tactics Die at start of Combat/Hazard Scene.
* **Perks:**
    1.  **Standard Issue:** Ignore "Clumsy" on Heavy Armor/Weapons.
    2.  **Grit:** Roll 4 Dice on Body Saves.
    3.  **Bandolier:** Spend 1 CP to reveal a specific combat consumable (Grenade/Ammo).

### B. [THE ESOTERIST](/02_Character_Creation/01_Archetypes/ESOTERIST/) (Arcana d8)
* **Resource:** Agony (Accrued Stress from casting).
* **Perks:**
    1.  **The Sight:** Detect magic/murk automatically.
    2.  **Mental Fortress:** Roll 4 Dice on Mind Saves.
    3.  **The Conduit:** Cast Spells for Stress (1d6/2d6/3d6 per Magnitude). Vent Agony to heal Stress (deals AoE dmg equal to Agony vented).

### C. [THE ACOLYTE](/02_Character_Creation/01_Archetypes/ACOLYTE/) (Devotion d8)
* **Resource:** Favor — Roll Devotion Die (d8) at session start. Cap = die max.
* **Perks:**
    1.  **The Covenant:** Only Acolytes can generate Favor and use the Petition system.
    2.  **Shepherd of the Lost:** Free lodging at faithful institutions. +1d6 on social checks to de-escalate violence.
    3.  **Confessor:** Talk to an NPC for a Scene → Director reveals one Secret, Fear, or Regret.

### D. [THE RUFFIAN](/02_Character_Creation/01_Archetypes/RUFFIAN/) (Guile d8)
* **Resource:** Trick Tokens — Gain 3 at session start; refresh 1 per Scene.
* **Perks:**
    1.  **Streetwise:** Locate black markets/fences instantly. No complications on Black Market transactions.
    2.  **Slippery:** Spend 1 Token to Re-Roll a failed Stress Save (Must keep 2nd).
    3.  **Scavenger's Eye:** Spend 1 Token to produce a common utility item from pockets.

## 3. CHOOSE SPECIES
* **[HUMAN](/02_Character_Creation/Divergences/DIVERGENCES/):** **The Spark.** +1 to Max Resource Cap.
* **[MURKBORN](/02_Character_Creation/Divergences/DIVERGENCES/):** **Fog-Lung.** Immune to Murk Toxicity/Lung Rot. **Stigma:** +2 Difficulty on Social checks vs Civilized.
* **[ANTHROPARION](/02_Character_Creation/Divergences/DIVERGENCES/):** **Plating.** Natural Armor 1 (Threshold +1 to worn armor). **Inorganic:** Med-kits fail; require Repair Kits instead.
* **[CHIMERA](/02_Character_Creation/Divergences/DIVERGENCES/):** **Beast Blood.** Choose: *Claws (d6 Unarmed)*, *Night-Eyes (see in darkness)*, or *Thick Hide (ignore environmental climate penalties)*. **Beacon:** Magic/Spirits target you first.

---

## 4. SELECT TALENTS
*Choose 1 Talent from your Archetype's Tier 1 tree. Additional Talents are purchased with XP. ([Full details: Talents, Boons & Burdens](/13_Talents_Boons_and_Flaws/))*

### GENERIC (Purchase with XP — 8 XP each)
* **Hard to Kill:** +3 Injury Pool.
* **Indomitable:** Re-roll failed Body Save (1/Scene).
* **Gunslinger:** Free Pistol attack before Initiative is determined; no melee penalty with pistols.
* **Field Chirurgeon:** Heal ally for 2 Mind Dice (Action). Requires Med-Kit.
* **Danger Sense:** Roll `2d6 + Attribute Die + Attribute Die` for Initiative.
* **Mule:** +4 Inventory Slots.
* **Pugilist:** Unarmed = d6 Dmg. Crit (6) knocks Prone.
* **Silver Tongue:** Roll 2d6 + Guile + Guile for Social checks.
* **Tinkerer:** Add +1d4 to Repair/Pick Lock/Hotwire rolls.
* **Iron Nerves:** Reduce Stress taken from fear, horror, or psychic sources by 2.

### ARCHETYPE (Pick 1 from Tier 1 only at creation — 12 XP value)
* **Soldier (Tier 1):** Suppressing Fire, Quartermaster, Sentry.
* **Esoterist (Tier 1):** Ritualist, Artificer, Soul Sight.
* **Acolyte (Tier 1):** Soothing Words, Guidance, Sanctuary.
* **Ruffian (Tier 1):** Pocket Sand, Gutter Runner, Sticky Fingers.

## 5. DERIVED STATS

| Stat | Formula |
| :--- | :--- |
| **INJURY POOL** | 3 × Body Die value (e.g., Body d8 = Pool 24). Breaking threshold. |
| **STRESS TRACK** | Starts at 0. Increases as you take hits. |
| **LOAD SLOTS** | Equal to Body Die value (e.g., Body d10 = 10 Slots). |
| **DEFENSE RATING (DR)** | 10 + (Body Die size ÷ 2). *Body d8 = DR 14. Body d10 = DR 15.* |

---

## 6. STARTING SKILLS
*Based on Mind Die. Each Trained Skill gives +2 to relevant checks.*

* **Mind d4/d6:** Choose **4** Trained Skills (+2).
* **Mind d8/d10:** Choose **5** Trained Skills (+2).
* **Mind d12:** Choose **6** Trained Skills (+2).

**Skill List:** Brawl, Melee, Small Arms, Long Guns, Heavy Weapons | Athletics, Pilot, Stealth, Physique | Investigation, Lore, Medicine, Tech, Survival, Scrounge | Insight, Intimidation, Parley, Willpower.

## 7. GEAR PACKAGE

**A. THE BREACHER**
* Shotgun (2d8, Near, Blast)
* Machete (2d6)
* Light Armor (Threshold 6)
* Ammo Pack (x1)

**B. THE SCOUT**
* Rifle (2d8, Far, 2-Handed)
* Knife (1d6, Hidden)
* Light Armor (Threshold 6)
* Climbing Gear (+2 Climb)

**C. THE DUELIST**
* Revolver (1d8, Near, Quick Draw) x2 *(If Gunslinger)* OR Revolver + Sword (2d6)
* Heavy Coat (Light Armor, Threshold 6)
* Med-Kit (3 Uses)

**D. THE ARCANIST (Esoterist Only)**
* Pistol (1d8, Near)
