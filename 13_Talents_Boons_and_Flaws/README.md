# CHAPTER 4: TALENTS, BOONS & BURDENS

## 1. TALENTS
At Character Creation, choose **1 Talent from your Archetype's Tier 1 tree**. Generic Talents can be purchased with XP during play (8 XP each), or gained by taking a Burden at creation.

### GENERIC TALENTS (PURCHASED WITH XP OR VIA BURDEN)
*Survival skills available to any professional.*

* **HARD TO KILL:** Increase your Stress Threshold (Injury Pool) by **+3**.
* **INDOMITABLE:** Once per Scene, if you fail a Body Save, you may **Re-Roll it** immediately. You must take the second result.
* **GUNSLINGER:** Your pistol work is reflexive — draw and fire before others have blinked. You may make a free Pistol attack at the very start of any combat, before Initiative is rolled. This does not cost your turn Action. Pistol attacks also never suffer the -2 penalty for firing in melee.
* **FIELD CHIRURGEON:** Spend an Action and use a Med-Kit on an adjacent ally. Roll **2 Mind Dice** (which Explode on max value). Remove that much Stress from the ally.
* **DANGER SENSE:** When rolling Initiative, roll `2d6 + Attribute Die + Attribute Die` (add your Attribute Die to the total twice, as Silver Tongue does for social checks).
* **MULE:** Increase your Load Slots by **+4**. You are never Encumbered by dragging a body.
* **PUGILIST:** Your Unarmed Attacks deal **d6 Damage** (instead of 1). If you hit a target with a Natural 6 on the die, they are knocked Prone.
* **SILVER TONGUE:** When making a Social Check (Lie, Persuade, Barter), you roll `2d6 + Guile Die + Guile Die`. (Add your stat to the total twice).
* **TINKERER:** When repairing gear, picking mechanical locks, or hotwiring vehicles, add **+1d4** to the roll.
* **IRON NERVES:** Your mind holds steady when others fracture. When you take Stress from fear, horror, or psychic sources (including the Whisper), reduce the Stress taken by **2** (minimum 0).



## 2. BOONS 
*Your history, contacts, and natural talents.*

**Step 1:** Choose **ONE** Free Boon.
**Step 2 (Optional):** Take a **Burden** to gain a 2nd Boon OR an extra Generic Talent.

### SOCIAL & CONNECTIONS
1.  **FRIENDS IN LOW PLACES:** You have a contact in every settlement. Once per Episode, instantly meet a Fence/Informant.
2.  **NOBLE REGALIA:** You have a title (or a convincing forgery). Authorities must grant you an audience and cannot "ignore" you (only refuse).
3.  **THE FIXER:** Re-Roll Barter checks (Keep second). Trade scrap for secrets at equal value.
4.  **LEGENDARY REPUTATION:** Make a Spirit Check upon entering a settlement. Success = Recognition, free drinks, thugs won't fight you.
5.  **VETERAN'S EYE (Experienced):** Before engaging with an enemy or entering a dangerous situation, you may ask the Director one direct question ("What's the biggest threat here?", "Is there another way out?", "What are they hiding?"). The Director must answer honestly.
6.  **OLD MONEY (Wealthy):** You start with **5 extra Resources**. You can stay in the High Districts for free (High standard of living) without paying upkeep.

### SURVIVAL & GRIT
7.  **IRON STOMACH:** Immune to ingested poison/disease. Can eat raw Murk-rat and drink swamp water safely.
8.  **JURY RIGGER:** Repair anything instantly without parts. It works for one Scene, then breaks permanently.
9.  **GHOST IN THE MACHINE:** Touch Old Tech to ask the Director one truthful question about it.
10. **BEAST WHISPERER:** Animals treat you as kin. You can give simple commands to them automatically.
11. **SIXTH SENSE (Perceptive):** You have a preternatural awareness of threat. Once per Episode, when the party is about to walk into a trap or ambush, the Director must give you one vague sensory warning before it triggers (a smell, a sound, a wrong feeling). You cannot identify the source — only that something is wrong.
12. **SCHOLAR:** You have a library in your head. Gain **+1d6** on checks to recall history, identify monsters, or decipher pre-war text.

### ACTION & COMBAT
13. **LIGHTFOOT:** Never roll for balance. Take 0 Stress from falls of 2 stories or less.
14. **DIE HARD:** If you break (Rank of Suffering), ignore "Unconscious" for one final round. You stay standing to act.
15. **DIRTY FIGHTING:** Improvised weapons deal d6 Damage. Deal **+1d6 Damage** to unaware targets.
16. **LINGUIST:** Speak Trade Tongue, High Dorian, and Old Vardan. Read ancient runes/ciphers.
17. **HEAVY LIFTER (Strong):** Your frame handles loads that stop others. Gain **+1d6** on Physique checks to break doors, lift debris, or resist being moved. When you drag an unconscious ally, you suffer no movement penalty and can still Rush.
18. **DEVIL'S LUCK (Narrow Escape):** Once per Session, when a scene would result in your capture, unconsciousness, or death by a hazard (not direct combat), declare a Narrow Escape. You survive with **d6 Stress** instead of the full consequence. Describe how you barely made it.

## 3. THE BURDENS 
*When your Burden causes significant trouble, you gain d6 Potential.*

### OBLIGATIONS & CURSES
1.  **VENDETTA:** A bounty hunter targets you. Once per Episode, the Director sends an assassin you cannot hide from.
2.  **DEBT:** You owe a Crime Lord. At the start of each Episode, the Director assigns you a job (a delivery, extraction, intimidation, or theft) that must be completed before the Episode ends. Refuse or fail: take **d6 Stress** and the debt grows. Complete it: the pressure eases — until next time.
3.  **HAUNTED:** Spirits target you first. You take **d4 Stress** if you are in total darkness.
4.  **OATHBOUND:** You live by a code (Pacifism, Honesty, Never Kill, Protection of the Weak). When you break your Oath, immediately lose **d6 Potential** and gain the **Tainted** condition for the rest of the Scene (-2 to Spirit checks, -1 to all rolls). You do not lose your ability to gain Potential going forward.
5.  **TECH-CURSED:** The DR for all checks involving mechanical devices, firearms, and Old Empire technology is increased by **+4**. A Botch on such a check still destroys or permanently jams the device.
6.  **JINXED (Unlucky):** You Botch on a natural **1 or 2** on your Attribute Die (instead of only 1), provided the check also fails. The rest of the Botch rules apply normally.

### PHYSIOLOGICAL & MENTAL FLAWS
7.  **THE SHAKES (Addiction):** -2 to Body/Mind rolls if you go 24 hours without your fix.
8.  **DISTINCTIVE STYLE:** You cannot blend in. Auto-Fail Stealth in crowds.
9.  **GLASS JAW (Frail):** When you take Physical Suffering, you are instantly **Stunned** (Lose Turn).
10. **MURK-MAGNET:** The DR for all Murk Exposure checks and Lung Rot saves is increased by **+4**.
11. **INFAMOUS:** -3 Penalty to Barter/Scrounge. Honest folk won't trade with you.
12. **BLEEDING HEART:** You cannot ignore a plea for help without passing a Spirit Save (DR 15).
13. **ILLITERATE:** You cannot read or write. Auto-Fail checks to decipher text.
14. **MONOCULAR (Missing Eye):** You suffer **-2** on ranged attacks beyond Close range.
15. **HOT HEADED (Impulsive):** If you are insulted or challenged, you must make a Spirit Save (DR 13) to avoid attacking immediately.
16. **GOLD FEVER (Greedy):** When you encounter visible treasure that no one is claiming, make a **Spirit Save (DR 13)**. On failure, you cannot willingly leave it — you must take it, or spend your Action each round arguing with yourself. On success, you may walk away; the struggle earns you **1d6 Potential**.
17. **LOUD (Clumsy):** You suffer **-2** on all Stealth checks.
