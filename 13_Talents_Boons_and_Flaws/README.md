# CHAPTER 4: TALENTS, BOONS & BURDENS

## 1. TALENTS
At Character Creation, you choose **1 Generic Talent** and **1 Archetype Talent**.

### GENERIC TALENTS (AVAILABLE TO ALL)
*Survival skills for any professional.*

* **HARD TO KILL:** Increase your Stress Threshold (Injury Pool) by **+3**.
* **INDOMITABLE:** Once per Scene, if you fail a Body Save, you may **Re-Roll it** immediately. You must take the second result.
* **GUNSLINGER:** You can fire two Pistols in a single Action. Make two separate Attack rolls with a **-2 Penalty** to each.
* **FIELD CHIRURGEON:** Spend an Action and use a Med-Kit on an adjacent ally. Roll **2 Mind Dice** (which Explode on max value). Remove that much Stress from the ally.
* **DANGER SENSE:** When rolling Initiative, roll your Attribute Die **twice** and keep the better Slot result.
* **MULE:** Increase your Load Slots by **+4**. You are never Encumbered by dragging a body.
* **PUGILIST:** Your Unarmed Attacks deal **d6 Damage** (instead of 1). If you hit a target with a Natural 6 on the die, they are knocked Prone.
* **SILVER TONGUE:** When making a Social Check (Lie, Persuade, Barter), you roll `2d6 + Guile Die + Guile Die`. (Add your stat to the total twice).
* **TINKERER:** Gain a flat **+2 Bonus** to any roll made to repair gear, pick mechanical locks, or hotwire vehicles.
* **LIGHT SLEEPER:** You are never Surprised while sleeping. You act first in the Blitz Slot if the party is ambushed during Camp.

### ARCHETYPE TALENTS

#### SOLDIER (Tactics)
* **SUPPRESSING FIRE (1 CP):** Spray a Zone. Enemies make a Body Save. Failure: Mobs take Double Damage. Elites are **Pinned** (Cannot Move, -2 to Attacks).
* **OVERWATCH (1 CP):** *Reaction.* Interrupt an enemy who moves or attacks with a Free Attack.
* **THE OLD GUARD:** Tell a war story during a Scene. One ally gains **+1d6** to a single roll in the next Scene.
* **QUARTERMASTER:** *Passive.* The party consumes **50% less Rations and Water**.
* **COMBAT DRILL (2 CP):** Bark orders. All allies gain **+1d6** to their next Attack Roll this turn.

#### ESOTERIST (Arcana)
* **PRINCIPLE OF KINETICS:**
    * *Mag 1:* Impact (2d6 Damage + Push).
    * *Mag 2:* Crush (Destroy Armor/Object).
    * *Mag 3:* Gravity Well (4d6 Damage to Zone).
* **PRINCIPLE OF ENTROPY:**
    * *Mag 1:* Rust (Open lock/Break gun).
    * *Mag 2:* Wither (3d4 Dmg + Weakened).
    * *Mag 3:* Decay (3d8 Dmg + Turn to Ash).
* **PRINCIPLE OF LUMINESCENCE:**
    * *Mag 1:* Flash (1d6 Dmg + Blind).
    * *Mag 2:* Laser (3d6 Dmg + Ignore Armor).
    * *Mag 3:* Arc Lightning (3d6 Dmg to 3 Targets).
* **RITUALIST:** *Passive.* Know two Utility Rituals (Silence, Message) that cost 0 Stress.
* **ARTIFICER:** *Passive.* Permanently invest Potential to bind spells into items (Kinetic Bullets, Entropic Grenades).

#### ACOLYTE (Devotion)
* **SANCTUARY (2 Favor):** Consecrate a campsite. Murk-Beasts cannot enter for 8 hours unless attacked.
* **CONFESSOR (1 Favor):** Compel an NPC to reveal a secret (Spirit Save to resist).
* **BEAR THE WITNESS (Reaction):** When an ally fails a Stress Save, you make the save for them. Success = They are safe. Failure = You take the Rank of Suffering.
* **RITE OF THE CENSER (1 Favor):** Fill a Zone with holy smoke. Allies gain **+2 Defense** against Ranged/Spirits.
* **EXORCISM (3 Favor):** *Action.* Spirit Roll vs DR 15. Success: Deal 2 Ranks of Suffering to Spirit/Undead. Failure: Take 3d6 Stress.

#### RUFFIAN (Guile)
* **POCKET SAND (1 Token):** Target is **Blinded** (-4 to rolls) until they clear their eyes.
* **THE SHIV (2 Tokens):** Attack a target who hasn't acted yet. Deal **+2d6 Damage** (Dice Explode on 6s).
* **STICKY FINGERS:** *Passive.* When Looting, find **+1 Token** and **+1 Random Item**.
* **GUTTER RUNNER:** *Passive.* Climb at full speed. Move through tight/occupied spaces without slowing.
* **SUCKER PUNCH (1 Token):** Out of combat, make a Critical Melee Attack (+1d6 Dmg) *before* Initiative is rolled.

## 2. BOONS (THE EDGE)
*Your history, contacts, and natural talents.*

**Step 1:** Choose **ONE** Free Boon.
**Step 2 (Optional):** Take a **Burden** to gain a 2nd Boon OR an extra Generic Talent.

### SOCIAL & CONNECTIONS
1.  **FRIENDS IN LOW PLACES:** You have a contact in every settlement. Once per Episode, instantly meet a Fence/Informant.
2.  **NOBLE REGALIA:** You have a title. Authorities must grant you an audience and cannot "ignore" you (only refuse).
3.  **THE FIXER:** Re-Roll Barter checks (Keep second). Trade scrap for secrets at equal value.
4.  **LEGENDARY REPUTATION:** Make a Spirit Check upon entering a settlement. Success = Recognition, free drinks, thugs won't fight you.

### SURVIVAL & GRIT
5.  **IRON STOMACH:** Immune to ingested poison/disease. Can eat raw Murk-rat and drink swamp water safely.
6.  **JURY RIGGER:** Repair anything instantly without parts. It works for one Scene, then breaks permanently.
7.  **GHOST IN THE MACHINE:** Touch Old Tech to ask the Director one truthful question about it.
8.  **BEAST WHISPERER:** Animals treat you as kin. You can give simple commands to them automatically.

### ACTION & COMBAT
9.  **LIGHTFOOT:** Never roll for balance. Take 0 Stress from falls of 2 stories or less.
10. **DIE HARD:** If you break (Rank of Suffering), ignore "Unconscious" for one final round. You stay standing to act.
11. **DIRTY FIGHTING:** Improvised weapons deal d6 Damage. Deal **+1d6 Damage** to unaware targets.
12. **LINGUIST:** Speak Trade Tongue, High Dorian, and Old Vardan. Read ancient runes/ciphers.

## 3. THE BURDENS (THE FLAW)
*When your Burden causes significant trouble, you gain d6 Potential.*

### OBLIGATIONS & CURSES
1.  **VENDETTA:** A bounty hunter targets you. Once per Episode, the Director sends an assassin you cannot hide from.
2.  **DEBT:** You owe a Crime Lord. Pay **1 Resource** per Episode or take **d6 Stress** (Thugs beat you).
3.  **HAUNTED:** Spirits target you first. You take **d4 Stress** if you are in total darkness.
4.  **OATHBOUND:** You have a code. Break it = Lose ALL Potential and gain none for the Episode.
5.  **TECH-CURSED:** If you roll a "1" on a machine/gun check, it breaks immediately.

### PHYSIOLOGICAL & MENTAL FLAWS
6.  **THE SHAKES (Addiction):** -2 to Body/Mind rolls if you go 24 hours without your fix.
7.  **DISTINCTIVE STYLE:** You cannot blend in. Auto-Fail Stealth in crowds.
8.  **GLASS JAW:** When you take Physical Suffering, you are instantly **Stunned** (Lose Turn).
9.  **MURK-MAGNET:** Roll twice (take worst) on checks to resist Murk/Lung Rot.
10. **INFAMOUS:** -3 Penalty to Barter/Scrounge. Honest folk won't trade with you.
11. **BLEEDING HEART:** You cannot ignore a plea for help without passing a Spirit Save (DR 15).
12. **ILLITERATE:** You cannot read or write. Auto-Fail checks to decipher text.
